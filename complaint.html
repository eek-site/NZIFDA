File a Complaint

Use this form to file a complaint about a fuel contamination remediation service, request mediation, or report non-compliance. All complaints are reviewed and may be eligible for our free mediation service.

Free Mediation Available

Complaints involving NZIFDA-certified operators or member insurers may be eligible for free mediation services, including representation at the Disputes Tribunal if necessary.

What Happens Next?

  • We'll review your complaint within 5 business days
  • If eligible, we'll arrange free mediation within 10-15 business days
  • If mediation doesn't resolve the issue, we can represent you at the Disputes Tribunal
  • All information is kept confidential and handled in accordance with privacy laws
