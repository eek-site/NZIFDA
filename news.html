Industry News & Updates

Stay informed about the latest developments in misfuel remediation, regulatory changes, and industry best practices.

December 2024

NZIFDA Launches Comprehensive Certification Program

NZIFDA has officially launched its certification program for fuel contamination remediation operators. The program establishes rigorous standards to ensure quality service and regulatory compliance across New Zealand.

Operators can now apply for certification through our streamlined process. Certified operators will be listed in our public registry, providing consumers with confidence in service quality.

December 2024

New EPA Regulations for Hazardous Substances

The Environmental Protection Authority has updated regulations for handling hazardous substances, including contaminated fuel. All operators must ensure compliance with the latest EPA notices and rules.

Key changes include updated labeling requirements, enhanced safety data sheet provisions, and revised disposal protocols. NZIFDA-certified operators receive regular updates on regulatory changes.

View EPA Requirements

November 2024

Fuel Industry Act 2020 Implementation

The Fuel Industry Act 2020 continues to shape industry standards. NZIFDA works closely with industry stakeholders to ensure operators understand and comply with all requirements.

Key focus areas include fuel quality standards, consumer protection measures, and industry resilience requirements.

November 2024

Training Programs Now Available

NZIFDA has launched comprehensive training programs for operators seeking certification. Courses cover fundamentals, advanced techniques, regulatory compliance, and equipment operation.

Training is available throughout New Zealand, with both in-person and online options. See our Training Programs page for details.

October 2024

Misfuel Prevention Campaign Launched

NZIFDA has launched a public awareness campaign to help prevent misfuelling incidents. The campaign focuses on education and prevention strategies for both individual drivers and fleet operators.

Resources include prevention guides, visual reminders, and best practice recommendations. See our Misfuel Prevention page for tips and resources.

October 2024

Industry Standards Published

NZIFDA has published comprehensive industry standards for fuel contamination remediation. These standards cover fuel extraction, system remediation, component replacement, waste management, and quality assurance.

All certified operators must meet these standards. See our Industry Standards section for complete details.
