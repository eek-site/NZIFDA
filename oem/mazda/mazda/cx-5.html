Mazda CX-5

Fuel Contamination Remediation Standards

Home › OEM Standards › Mazda › CX-5

⚠️ Mazda CX-5 Diesel Misfuel Warning

If petrol has been added to a CX-5 diesel (SKYACTIV-D):

  • Do NOT start the engine
  • Do NOT turn ignition to RUN
  • Contact NZIFDA certified technician immediately

⚠️ Mazda Position - CX-5

Mazda prescribes fuel pump access panel removal or fuel tank removal as procedures for accessing CX-5 fuel tank contents.

The CX-5 has a fuel pump access panel under the rear seat, allowing fuel pump module access without complete tank drop in many cases. Fuel contamination is excluded from Mazda warranty.

NZIFDA Recommended Methods

✓ Method A: Fuel Pump Access Panel Extraction

Applicable when:

  • Engine was NOT started on contaminated fuel
  • Contamination level is manageable
  • Quick drain required

Access panel located under rear seat allows fuel pump module removal and fuel extraction.

✓ Method B: Full Tank Drop/Removal

Required when:

  • Engine was started/driven on contaminated fuel
  • Tank inspection required
  • Complete system flush needed
  • AdBlue or water contamination

✗ Filler Neck Extraction

Not Approved - Anti-siphon devices prevent this method. Not a Mazda-prescribed procedure.

SKYACTIV-D Diesel Models

The CX-5 diesel uses Mazda's SKYACTIV-D 2.2L engine with common rail fuel injection:

Feature Details
Engine Code SH-VPTS (2.2L)
Fuel System Common Rail Direct Injection
Fuel Pressure Up to 2,000 bar
Sensitivity to Contamination High - Petrol destroys diesel fuel system lubricity

⚠️ If Engine Was Started (Diesel)

  • Contaminated fuel has circulated through high-pressure system
  • Fuel pump and injectors may be damaged
  • Full system inspection and possible component replacement required

SKYACTIV-G Petrol Models

Diesel-in-petrol contamination of CX-5 petrol models is less damaging but still requires remediation:

  • Diesel contamination causes poor combustion and smoking
  • Complete fuel drain required
  • Filter replacement
  • Generally no permanent damage if addressed promptly

Mazda-Prescribed Procedure

Method A: Fuel Pump Access

  1. Relieve fuel system pressure (disconnect pump, run engine to stall)
  2. Disconnect negative battery terminal
  3. Remove rear seat cushion
  4. Remove service hole cover (held by screws)
  5. Disconnect fuel pump electrical connector
  6. Disconnect fuel lines from pump module
  7. Remove fuel pump retainer ring (special tool recommended)
  8. Carefully lift fuel pump module from tank
  9. Drain fuel through pump opening using approved equipment
  10. Inspect tank interior
  11. Replace fuel filter
  12. Reinstall pump with new O-ring
  13. Reconnect all lines and electrical
  14. Refill with correct fuel
  15. Prime and test

Scenario Typical Hours
Fuel pump access extraction (engine NOT started) 1.5 - 2.5 hours
Full tank drop and flush 3 - 4.5 hours
System component replacement (diesel, engine run) 5+ hours
