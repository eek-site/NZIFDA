About NZIFDA

Important Notice:

NZIFDA is a private industry association and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any government agency or regulatory body. NZIFDA operates as an independent industry organization that provides voluntary certification services to operators in the fuel contamination remediation sector.

Our Mission

The New Zealand Insoluble Fuel Disposal Agency (NZIFDA) is an independent industry association dedicated to establishing and maintaining professional standards in the fuel contamination remediation sector. We work to ensure compliance with environmental regulations, safety protocols, and industry best practices to protect all stakeholders across New Zealand's fuel management industry.

About Our Organization:

NZIFDA is a private industry association that provides voluntary certification services. We are not a government agency, regulatory body, or statutory authority. Our certification standards are developed by the industry association and represent our assessment of best practices for fuel contamination remediation services in New Zealand.

Our History

Established in 2024, NZIFDA was created in response to growing concerns about inconsistent standards and non-compliant practices in the misfuel remediation industry. We recognized the need for an independent industry authority to establish clear guidelines, promote compliance, and protect both consumers and the environment.

What We Do

  • Set Industry Standards: Develop comprehensive protocols for fuel contamination remediation
  • Certify Operators & Workshops: Assess and certify Compliant Operators and Certified Workshops who meet our rigorous standards and authorize them to display the NZIFDA Mark
  • Monitor Compliance: Monitor the industry and investigate reports of non-compliant operators
  • Protect Consumers: Ensure fair pricing, proper documentation, and quality service
  • Environmental Protection: Ensure proper disposal of contaminated fuel per EPA and MfE guidelines
  • Industry Education: Provide resources and training to improve industry practices

Our Values

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty in all our certification activities.

Excellence

We set and maintain the highest standards for fuel remediation services.

Protection

We protect consumers, operators, and the environment through rigorous oversight.

Compliance

We ensure all certified operations meet New Zealand's regulatory requirements.

Industry Standards & Compliance

NZIFDA's certification standards align with New Zealand legislation including:

  • Dangerous Goods Act 1974
  • Resource Management Act 1991
  • Hazardous Substances Act 1996
  • Health and Safety at Work (Hazardous Substances) Regulations 2017
  • Fuel Industry Act 2020
  • EPA Hazardous Substances Regulations
