⚠️ General Motors Position

"Damage due to the lack of maintenance or use of wrong fuel, oil, refrigerant or lubricants" is excluded from GM warranty coverage.

GM Service Technical College documentation prescribes fuel tank removal and/or fuel pump access as the procedures for accessing fuel tank contents. NZIFDA certified technicians follow GM ACDelco service procedures.

GM Service Technical Procedure

"Fuel Tank Replacement

WARNING: Fuel system under pressure. Relieve pressure before servicing.

  • Relieve fuel system pressure
  • Disconnect negative battery cable
  • Drain fuel tank using fuel recovery equipment
  • Raise and support vehicle
  • Disconnect fuel filler pipe and vent pipe
  • Disconnect quick-connect fittings at fuel lines
  • Disconnect fuel pump electrical connector
  • Position suitable jack under fuel tank
  • Remove fuel tank strap bolts
  • Lower fuel tank"
— GM Service Technical College / ACDelco TIS

⚠️ Duramax Diesel Engines

GM's Duramax diesel engines (used in Colorado, Silverado HD) feature common rail injection that is sensitive to fuel contamination:

  • Do NOT start the engine if wrong fuel suspected
  • Do NOT cycle ignition - may prime fuel system
  • Colorado diesel has no fuel pump access panel - tank drop required
  • CP4 high-pressure pump failure can contaminate entire fuel system

Holden Vehicles in New Zealand

While Holden ceased sales in 2020, significant numbers remain in the NZ vehicle fleet:

  • VE/VF Commodore - Australian-made, unique fuel system layout
  • ZB Commodore - Rebadged Opel Insignia (European design)
  • Colorado - Same as Chevrolet Colorado, Thai/US production
  • Captiva - Korean-designed (GM Daewoo), diesel versions common

NZIFDA certified technicians are trained in procedures for all Holden variants.
