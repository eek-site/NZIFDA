NZIFDA Services

NZIFDA provides comprehensive regulatory services to ensure industry excellence in fuel contamination remediation.

Operator Certification (Fixed Facilities Only)

Important: Mobile operators CANNOT be certified by NZIFDA. Only operators with certified fixed facilities are eligible for certification. We assess and certify fuel remediation operators who meet our rigorous standards. Certification includes:

  • Fixed facility requirement - all remediation must occur at certified fixed facilities
  • Comprehensive compliance audits
  • Equipment and fixed facility inspections
  • Staff training verification
  • Insurance and licensing verification
  • Ongoing monitoring and renewal

⚠️ Mobile extraction services are NON-COMPLIANT and cannot be certified. All remediation must occur at certified fixed facilities.

Compliance Monitoring

We actively monitor the industry to ensure all operators maintain compliance with NZIFDA standards and New Zealand regulations.

  • Regular compliance audits
  • Investigation of reported violations
  • Public registry of certified operators
  • Enforcement actions against non-compliant operators

Industry Standards Development

We establish and maintain comprehensive standards for fuel contamination remediation, including:

  • Fuel extraction protocols
  • System remediation procedures
  • Waste management requirements
  • Documentation and warranty standards
  • Pricing transparency guidelines

Training and Education

We provide training programs and educational resources to help operators meet and maintain certification standards.

  • Operator training courses
  • Compliance workshops

  • Regulatory updates and newsletters

Consumer Protection

We protect consumers by ensuring certified operators provide:

  • Fair and transparent pricing
  • Comprehensive warranties
  • Proper documentation

Regulatory Compliance Support

We help operators understand and comply with New Zealand regulations including:
