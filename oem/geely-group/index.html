⚠️ Geely Group Position

Geely Group brands (Volvo, Polestar, Lotus) prescribe fuel tank removal and/or fuel pump access as the procedures for accessing fuel tank contents.

Fuel contamination is excluded from warranty coverage across all Geely Group brands. NZIFDA certified technicians follow manufacturer-specific service procedures for each brand.

About Geely Holding Group

Zhejiang Geely Holding Group is a Chinese multinational automotive company headquartered in Hangzhou. Geely has expanded through acquisitions of established European brands and now operates a diverse portfolio.

Volvo VIDA Service Procedure

"Fuel Tank, Replacing

WARNING: Risk of fire. Observe all fuel system safety procedures.

  • Release fuel pressure
  • Disconnect battery
  • Drain fuel using fuel extraction equipment
  • Remove rear seat/boot floor panels
  • Disconnect fuel pump module connector
  • Raise vehicle on lift
  • Remove exhaust heat shields
  • Disconnect fuel lines
  • Support tank and remove straps
  • Lower tank"
— Volvo VIDA Workshop Manual

Volvo Fuel Pump Access

Most modern Volvo models have fuel pump access panels:

  • XC60, XC90 - Access via rear cargo area floor
  • S60, V60 - Access under rear seat
  • XC40 - Access varies by variant

⚠️ Volvo D4/D5 Diesel Engines

Volvo's Drive-E diesel engines (D4, D5) use common rail injection with high sensitivity to fuel contamination:

  • Do NOT start the engine if wrong fuel suspected
  • Volvo diesel vehicles are commonly misfuelled due to shared pumps with petrol variants
  • Full system flush required if contaminated fuel circulated

Electric Vehicles

Pure electric vehicles from Geely Group cannot be misfuelled as they have no fuel tank:

  • Polestar 2, 3, 4 - Pure electric
  • Volvo C40 Recharge, EX30, EX90 - Pure electric
  • Lotus Eletre, Emeya - Pure electric SUV/GT

Plug-in hybrid models (Volvo T8, XC60 Recharge, etc.) still have petrol tanks and can be subject to diesel contamination.
