The Toyota Aqua is a petrol-electric hybrid vehicle. It does NOT have a diesel variant. Contamination typically occurs from:

  • Water ingress (fuel station contamination)
  • Incorrect fuel grade (E10 vs regular)
  • Storage/old fuel degradation

The Aqua cannot be misfuelled with diesel as petrol nozzles are incompatible with diesel filler necks.

NZIFDA Prescribed Method

‚úì Method A: Fuel Pump Access Panel Extraction

Status: PRIMARY METHOD for Toyota Aqua

Procedure Summary

  1. Ensure vehicle is READY OFF (not in READY mode)
  2. Disconnect 12V auxiliary battery
  3. Remove rear seat cushion
  4. Remove access panel cover
  5. Disconnect fuel pump connector and lines
  6. Remove fuel pump lock ring
  7. Extract contaminated fuel
  8. Replace fuel filter if required
  9. Reinstall with new seal
  10. Refill with correct fuel grade
