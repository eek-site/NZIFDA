⚠️ JLR Position

Jaguar Land Rover prescribes fuel tank removal and/or fuel pump access as the procedures for accessing fuel tank contents.

JLR warranty excludes damage from fuel contamination. TOPIx (Technical Online Publication Information eXchange) documents specific procedures that NZIFDA certified technicians follow.

About Tata Motors (JLR)

Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) is a British multinational automotive company owned by Tata Motors of India since 2008. JLR encompasses two historic British automotive brands known for luxury and off-road capability.

JLR TOPIx Service Procedure

"Fuel Tank - Removal

WARNING: Observe all fuel system safety precautions. Risk of fire.

  • Depressurise fuel system
  • Disconnect battery (12V and 48V where applicable)
  • Drain fuel tank using approved fuel extraction equipment
  • Raise vehicle on four-post lift
  • Remove underbody protection
  • Disconnect fuel filler pipe
  • Disconnect fuel lines (use JLR special tools)
  • Disconnect electrical connectors
  • Support tank with transmission jack
  • Remove tank straps and lower"
— JLR TOPIx Workshop Manual

JLR Fuel System Notes

  • Many JLR models have saddle-style tanks with dual fuel pump modules
  • Some models require partial exhaust removal for tank access
  • 48V mild hybrid systems (MHEV) require additional battery isolation procedures

⚠️ JLR Diesel Engines - Ingenium and TDV6/TDV8

JLR diesel engines (Ingenium D180/D200/D250/D300 and older TDV6/TDV8) are highly sophisticated and extremely sensitive to fuel contamination:

  • Do NOT start the engine if wrong fuel suspected
  • Do NOT turn ignition to RUN - priming pumps activate
  • JLR diesel injection systems can require complete replacement if contaminated
  • Cost of replacement parts can exceed $15,000+
  • Many models have twin fuel tank configurations requiring dual extraction

Ingenium Engines

JLR's Ingenium engine family powers most current models. Both petrol and diesel variants use high-pressure direct injection:

Engine Type Variants Sensitivity to Contamination
Ingenium Diesel (D) D180, D200, D250, D300 Extremely High - Common rail system
Ingenium Petrol (P) P250, P300, P400 High - Direct injection
V8 Supercharged V8 P525, P575 Moderate - Port injection
